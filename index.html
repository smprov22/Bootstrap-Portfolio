About Me

My name is Stephanie Provence and I'm taking this coding bootcamp to learn new skills and hopefully enter a new career path. Although I expect this to be very challenging, I think the reward of a new, fulfilling career will be well worth it in the long run.

For the last 10 years I lived in Los Angeles, pursuing an editing career in the TV/Film industry. Although I did ok down there, I never made it as far as I wanted, and after having my daughter in 2017, my husband and I decided that living near family while my daughter is young is more important than working in the film industry so we moved up to Sacramento.
